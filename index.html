üí¨ Our Interviews

Q: Where do you shop?

Alex: I go to the supermarket with my family.

Q: Why do you like it there?

Alex: Because they have lots of food and it's near my home. The prices are good!

Q: When do you go shopping?

Alex: Usually on Saturday mornings with my mom.

Q: What do you buy?

Alex: Food like milk, bread, and fruit. Sometimes snacks and juice!

Q: Where do you like to shop?

Sofia: I like the shopping mall! And I shop online sometimes too.

Q: What's good about these places?

Sofia: The mall has cool shops with clothes and games. Online shopping is easy - I can order from home!

Q: When do you shop?

Sofia: After school, on weekends, and during holidays. I like shopping with my friends!

Q: What do you buy?

Sofia: Clothes, shoes, books, and games. I love getting new stuff!

Q: Where do you shop?

Marcus: I go to the small market near my house.

Q: Why do you choose the market?

Marcus: Because the fruit and vegetables are fresh and cheap! I know the people who work there.

Q: When do you go?

Marcus: In the morning before school. Sometimes with my grandma.

Q: What do you usually buy?

Marcus: Fruit, vegetables, bread, and sometimes cheese. My grandma likes fresh food!

üìå What Did We Learn?

  • Different Places: Some people like supermarkets because they have everything. Others like markets for fresh food or shopping malls for clothes.
  • Why People Choose: People choose places because they like the prices, the quality, how close it is, or the friendly people there.
  • When We Shop: Some shop in the morning, some in the afternoon, and some on weekends. It depends on what we like!
  • What We Buy: Everyone buys food, but some people also like clothes, games, and books. It's different for everyone!
